TFisoDB

TFisoDB is a database of the molecular properties of alternative isoforms of transcription factors. We have systematically tested a clone collection of 693 isoforms of 246 TF genes in assays assessing DNA binding, protein-protein interactions, transcriptional activation, localization, and condensate formation. Read the preprint describing our findings here.

TFisoDB is a collaboration between the Fuxman Bass, Bulyk, Sahni, and Salomonis, and Vidal labs
